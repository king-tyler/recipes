# Peanut Butter Balls

![](/recipes/images/peanut-butter-balls.jpg)

## Ingredients

- **1 cup** corn syrup
- **1 cup** sugar
- **1 1/4 cup** creamy peanut butter
- **5 cups** Cocoa Krispies

## Directions

Mix corn syrup and sugar together and microwave. Mix again after two minutes and continue
microwaving until the sugar is brought to a boil.

Immediately, add peanut butter and mix. Add Cocoa Krispies one cup at a time. Make into balls using
a cookie scoop or spoon and put on greased cookie sheet or wax paper.
