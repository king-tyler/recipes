# Mexican Rice Dish

![](/recipes/images/mexican-rice-dish.jpg)

## Ingredients

- **12 oz** U-shaped smoked sausage
- **2 cups** frozen corn
- **1 can** Rotel tomatoes
- **1 cup** cooked rice
- **2 tsp** chili powder
- **1/2 tsp** cumin
- **1 can** pinto beans, drained and rinsed
- **1 can** black beans, drained and rinsed
- cheddar cheese
- Fritos Scoops

## Directions

In a large skillet, brown sausage over medium heat. Drain off any excess fat. Add rest of the
ingredients (except cheese and Fritos) and heat through. Serve with cheese sprinkled on top. Use
Fritos Scoops to eat.
