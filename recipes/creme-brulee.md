# Crème brûlée

## Ingredients

- **1 pint** heavy cream
- **6** egg yolks
- **1/2 cup** sugar
- **1/4 tsp** salt
- **2 tsp** vanilla

## Directions

Sous vide at 176°F for 1 to 1 1/2 hours, mixing at the half-way mark. Poor into ramekins and
refrigerate overnight. Sprinkle with sugar and carmelize with a flame torch.
