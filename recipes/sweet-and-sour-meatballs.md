# Sweet & Sour Meatballs

![](/recipes/images/sweet-and-sour-meatballs.jpg)

## Ingredients

- **1 1/2 lb** meatballs
- **2 tbsp** cornstarch
- **1/2 cup** brown sugar
- **1 can** crushed pineapple (reserve syrup)
- **1/3 cup** vinegar
- **1 tbsp** soy sauce
- **1** green pepper

## Directions

Broil green pepper, turning until skin darkens. Peel the pepper and dice.

Mix cornstarch and sugar. Stir in reserved pineapple syrup, vinegar, and soy sauce until smooth.

Mix meatballs, crushed pineapple, green pepper, and sauce in a in crockpot and cook until heated
through.
