# Basic Hamburger

## Ingredients

- **1** egg
- **1/2 tsp** pepper
- **1 lb** ground beef
- **1/2 cup** oats

## Directions

In a medium bowl, mix together egg, pepper and oats. Place ground beef into the mixture. With hands
or a fork, mix until well blended. Form into 150 gram patties (or 75 grams for sliders).
