# Sausage Potato Soup

![](/recipes/images/sausage-potato-soup.jpg)

## Ingredients

- **3 strips** thick cut bacon, chopped
- **2 lb** ground hot Italian sausage
- **1** large onion
- **1 tsp** oregano
- **4** garlic cloves, minced
- **4-6** medium potatoes
- **6 cups** chicken broth
- **1 bunch** kale, chopped
- **1 - 1 1/2 cups** heavy cream

## Directions

Turn the Instant Pot to Saut√© mode and add the chopped bacon. Cook, stirring occasionally, until
the bacon renders out most of its fat. Remove and set aside.

Add the sausage and brown it, stirring a few times. Add onion and oregano. When the onion start to
turn translucent, add the garlic. Pour in the broth, stirring and scraping the bottom. Add
potatoes and bacon.

Pressure cook for 5 minutes with an additional 10 minutes NPR. Release steam.

Stir in kale, cover and let sit for 5 minutes. Stir in heavy cream
