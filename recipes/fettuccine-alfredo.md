# Fettuccine Alfredo

![](/recipes/images/fettuccine-alfredo.jpg)

## Ingredients

- **1/2 cup** butter
- **1 oz** cream cheese
- **1 pint** heavy cream
- **1 tsp** garlic powder
- **2/3 cup** grated parmesan cheese
- **1 lb** pasta

## Directions

In a medium saucepan, melt butter. When butter is melted, add cream cheese. When the cream cheese
is softened, add heavy cream. Season with garlic powder and pepper.

Simmer for 15-20 minutes over low heat, stirring constantly. Remove from heat and stir in parmesan.
Serve over hot fettucine noodles.
