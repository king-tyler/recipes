# Hawaiian Haystacks

## Ingredients

- **2 1/2 cups** milk
- **3 tbsp** cornstarch
- **3 tbsp** butter
- **1** chicken bouillon cube
- salt and pepper, to taste
- **1/2 tsp** onion powder
- **1 tsp** garlic powder
- **1 cup** water
- **3 cups** chicken (2 large breasts), cooked and shredded
- **1 1/2 cups** rice

### Toppings

- Pineapple
- Chow mein noodles
- Green onion
- Shredded cheese
- Diced tomatoes
- Celery
- Bell peppers
- Peanuts

## Directions

Cook rice.

In a small saucepan, whisk together milk, water and cornstarch. Add butter, bouillon, salt, pepper,
onion powder, and garlic powder.

Bring to a low boil, stirring frequently. Reduce heat and cook until the sauce has thickened to
gravy consistency. Add shredded chicken.

Server over rice and add desired toppings.
