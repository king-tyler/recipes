# Peanut Butter Chocolate Pie

![](/recipes/images/peanut-butter-chocolate-pie.jpg)

## Ingredients

- **1** graham cracker pie crust

### Chocolate Filling

- **1/2 cup** butter, softened
- **1/2 cup** powdered sugar
- **1** egg
- **3/4 cup** semi-sweet chocolate chips, melted
- **3/4 tsp** vanilla
- **1/2 cup** finely chopped peanuts

### Peanut Butter Filling

- **8 oz** cream cheese, softened
- **1/2 cup** creamy peanut butter
- **1 cup** powdered sugar
- **1** egg
- **1 1/2 cups** whipped cream
- **1/4 cup** finely chopped peanuts

## Directions

In a small bowl, beat butter and powdered sugar until light and fluffy. Add egg; beat at medium
speed 2 to 3 minutes. Blend in melted chocolate and vanilla; mix well. Spread 1 cup chocolate
filling evenly into pie crust (reserve remaining chocolate filling for top of pie). Sprinkle with
chopped peanuts.

In a large bowl, combine cream cheese and peanut butter at medium speed until well blended. Add
powdered sugar and egg; beat until smooth and creamy. Fold in whipped cream. Spoon peanut butter
filling evenly over chocolate filling. Spread remaining chocolate filling in a circle over top of
pie to within 2 inches of edge. Sprinkle chopped peanuts over peanut butter filling. Refrigerate at
least 3 hours before serving. Store in refrigerator.
