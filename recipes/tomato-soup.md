# Tomato Soup

## Ingredients

- **2 lb (16)** roma tomatoes
- **1** medium onion
- **8** garlic cloves
- **4 cups** chicken broth
- **1/4 cup** butter
- **1/4 cup** flour
- **4 tsp** sugar
- **2 tbsp** Italian seasoning

## Directions

In a large pot, cook tomatoes, onion, garlic and broth over medium heat for 20 minutes. Blend using
an immersion blender until smooth. Add butter and whisk flour. Season with salt, sugar, and Italian
seasoning.
