# Chicken Salad Sandwich

![](/recipes/images/chicken-salad-sandwich.jpg)

## Ingredients

- **4 cups** cooked chicken, shredded
- **3** stalks celery, chopped
- **1 cup** sliced grapes
- **1/2 cups** chopped pecans
- **1 1/2 cups** mayo
- **1/3 cup** honey
- **1/4 tsp** salt
- **1/4 tsp** black pepper
- **8 - 10** small croissants

## Directions

Combine chicken, celery, grapes, and pecans in a bowl. In a separate bowl, whisk together mayo,
honey, salt, and pepper. Combine dressing with chicken mixture. Serve on croissants or bread of
choice.
