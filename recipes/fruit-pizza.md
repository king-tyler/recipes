# Fruit Pizza

![](/recipes/images/fruit-pizza.jpg)

## Ingredients

### Crust

- **1 box** yellow cake mix
- **2 tbsp** water
- **1** egg
- **2 tbsp** butter
- **2 tbsp** brown sugar

### Toppings

- **12 oz** cream cheese, softened
- **1/2 cup** sugar
- **1 tsp** vanilla
- Fruits of choice (strawberries, oranges, grapes, pineapple, blueberries, raspberries, etc.)

## Directions

Combine the crust ingredients and press into a greased pizza pan. Do not make an edge. Press
completely flat or mixture will cook over the edge and onto your oven. Bake at 375ÂºF for 15
minutes.

Mix well the softened cream cheese, sugar and vanilla and spread over warm crust. When cool,
arrange fruit over pizza.
