# Pancakes

## Ingredients

### Pancake Mix

- **1 cup** flour
- **1 cup** whole wheat flour
- **1 tsp** baking soda
- **1 tsp** baking powder
- **1 tsp** salt
- **2 tsp** sugar
- **2 tsp** cinnamon

### Pancake Batter

- **2 cup** pancake mix
- **4** eggs
- **2 tsp** vanilla extract
- **4** mashed bananas or **1 1/2 cup** apple sauce or **3/4 cup** sugar
- **2-6 tbsp** water or milk

## Directions

Combine pancake mix, then add wet ingredients. Add water or milk until the batter reaches a
slightly running consistency. Cook on a griddle at about 325Â°F for 3-4 minutes on each side.
