# Pork Nachos

![](/recipes/images/pork-nachos.jpg)

## Ingredients

- **1** boneless pork roast
- **2 cans** pinto beans
- **1 can** black beans, drained and rinsed
- **2 cans** diced green chiles
- **1 can** Rotel tomatoes
- **1 tsp** pepper
- **2 tsp** chili powder
- **2 tsp** cumin
- **2 tsp** garlic salt

## Directions

Put pork roast in crockpot. Pour all ingredients over top of roast. Cook on low for 8 hours. Shred
meat with fork. Serve over tortilla chips or in flour tortillas.
