# Peanut Butter Fingers

![](/recipes/images/peanut-butter-fingers.jpg)

## Ingredients

- **1 cup** butter
- **1 cup** sugar
- **1 cup** brown sugar
- **2** egg
- **2/3 cup** peanut butter
- **1 tsp** baking soda
- **1/2 tsp** salt
- **1 tsp** vanilla
- **2 cup** flour
- **2 cup** oats
- **2 cup** chocolate chips

### Frosting

- **1 cup** powdered sugar
- **1/2 cup** peanut Butter
- **1/4 - 1/2 cup** milk or heavy cream

## Directions

Cream butter, sugar and brown sugar. Blend in egg, peanut butter, baking soda, salt and vanilla.
Stir in flour and oats.

Spread in 13"x9" pan. Bake at 350Â°F for 19 minutes. Sprinkle with chocolate chips while still in
oven. When soft, spread evenly.

Combine powdered sugar, peanut butter, and milk. Drizzle and spread over chocolate. Cool and cut
into bars.
