# Muddy Buddies

![](/recipes/images/muddy-buddies.jpg)

## Ingredients

- **1/2 cup** butter
- **3/4 cup** peanut butter
- **2 cups** semi-sweet chocolate chips
- **1 (12 oz)** box of rice Chex
- **3-4 cups** powdered sugar

## Directions

In a large microwavable bowl, melt and stir the butter and chocolate chips. Mix in the peanut
butter. In a large container, add the box of Chex and stir in mixture to coat. Add powdered sugar,
close container, and shake well until all pieces are coated.
