# Four-Color Cookies

## Ingredients

- **1 1/2 cups** powdered sugar
- **2 sticks** unsalted butter
- **1** egg
- **1/2 tsp** vanilla
- **1/2 tsp** salt
- **3 cups** flour
- **1 tbsp** milk
- Food coloring & Cocoa

## Directions

Cream powdered sugar and butter until smooth. Add egg and mix thoroughly. Add vanilla. Sift
together salt and flour. Add flour mixture to sugar mixture alternating with the milk. Mix about
2 minutes to create a smooth dough. Divide into four portions and color one portion red, one
portion green, one portion with cocoa, and the last uncolored. Roll each portion into a log
approximately 1 1/4" in diameter. Wrap in wax paper and refigerate overnight.

Cut each log lengthwise into four quarters. Reassemble quarters into full logs with different
colored quarters put together. Re-roll log slightly and store them wrapped in the refrigerator.

When ready to bake, cut logs into 1/4" segments and bake at 325Â°F for 10 to 12 minutes.
