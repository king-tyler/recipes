# Empanadas

![](/recipes/images/empanadas.jpg)

## Ingredients

### Dough

- **490 g** flour
- **1/4 cup** butter
- **1 cup** boiling water
- **2 tsp** salt
- **1 tbsp** sugar

### Filling

- **1** medium onion
- **1** medium pepper
- **3** garlic cloves
- **1 tsp** cumin
- **1 tsp** paprika
- **1 tsp** rosemary
- **1 tsp** thyme
- **2 tbsp** soy sauce
- **1 tbsp** tomato paste
- **2 tbsp** balsamic vinegar
- **1/2 lb** ground beef
- **14 oz** kielbasa sausage

### Brush

- **1** egg
- **1 tbps** milk

## Directions

Prepare filling up to a day before. Chop and fry the onion and pepper. Add the minced garlic. Add
remaining ingredients and cook meet through.

Roll out the dough using a pasta machine and cut into circles. Wet the outside of the circles. Fill
with a spoonfull of filling. Close the empanadas and crimp the edges with a fork

Brush with egg milk mixture. Place empanadas on a **greased** baking sheet

Cook for 25 minutes at 340Â°F.
