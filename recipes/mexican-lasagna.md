# Mexican Lasagna

![](/recipes/images/mexican-lasagna.jpg)

## Ingredients

- **3 tbsp** olive oil
- **2 lb** ground beef
- **2 tbsp** chili powder
- **2 tsp** cumin
- **1/2** onion, chopped
- **1 cup** black beans, drained and rinsed
- **1 cup** salsa
- **1 cup** frozen corn
- **8** flour tortillas

## Directions

Preheat the oven to 425ÂºF.

Preheat a large skillet over medium high heat coat with olive oil. Add meat and season with chili
powder, cumin, and red onion. Brown the meat, 5 minutes. Add salsa, black beans, and corn. Heat the
mixture through for 2 to 3 minutes, then season with salt to your taste.

Coat a shallow baking dish with 1 tbsp. olive oil. Cut the tortillas in half or quarters to make
them easy to layer. Build lasagna in layers of meat and beans, then tortillas, then cheese.
Repeat: meat, tortilla cheese again. Bake lasagna 12 to 15 minutes until cheese is brown and
bubbly. Top with scallions and serve.
