# Skillet Lasagna

![](/recipes/images/skillet-lasagna.jpg)

## Ingredients

- **1 lb** ground beef
- **1 (12 oz)** package bow tie noodles
- **1 (26 oz)** jar spaghetti sauce
- **1 tbsp** olive oil
- **1 tsp** salt
- **1/2 tsp** garlic powder
- **1 tsp** Italian seasoning
- **1/2 cup** shredded mozzarella cheese
- **1 dollop** sour cream

## Directions

Brown ground beef in a small pan over medium heat. Meanwhile, cook noodles according to package
directions. After noodles are cooked, drain off water and drizzle noodles with olive oil. Mix in
spaghetti sauce. Add browned beef, seasoning, cheese, and sour cream. Fold together and allow it
all to combine and heat through over low heat for about 5 minutes, or until cheese is melted.
