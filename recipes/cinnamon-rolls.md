# Cinnamon Rolls

## Ingredients

### Magic Dough

- **1/2 cup** butter, melted
- **1 tbsp** yeast
- **1 1/4 cups** warm water 
- **1 1/4 tsp** salt
- **3/8 cup** sugar
- **2** eggs
- **3 3/4 cups** flour

### Filling

- **3 tbsp** butter, melted
- **1/2 cup** brown sugar
- **1 1/2 tbsp** cinnamon

### Icing

- **2 tbsp** butter, softened
- **1 cup** powdered sugar
- **1 tsp** vanilla
- **1-2 tbsp** milk
- **pinch** of salt

## Directions

### Magic Dough

Dissolve yeast in water. Add sugar, salt, butter and
eggs. Mix flour, blending well. Put in large bowl, cover and **refrigerate overnight**.

Preheat the oven to 350°F.

Rollout dough into a rectangle on a heavily floured surface. Spread with butter. Sprinkle cinnamon
brown sugar mixture evenly.

Roll the dough into a cylinder. Cut into 12-15 cinnamon rolls and place in a 9"x13" greased baking
pan. Let it rise for 20-30 minutes. Bake for 20-25 minutes at 350°F until golden brown. Cover with
icing.
