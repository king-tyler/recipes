# Bean Dump Soup

![](/recipes/images/bean-dump-soup.jpg)

## Ingredients

- **1 1/2 lb** ground chicken or turkey
- **3 tbps** taco seasoning
- **1-2 cans** Rotel tomatoes
- **1 can** corn
- **1 can** black beans, drained and rinsed
- **1 can** kidney beans, drained and rinsed
- **1 can** garbanzo beans
- cheddar cheese
- Fritos Scoops

## Directions

Brown the ground meat and drain. Combine all ingredients into either a pot and heat through on the
stovetop, or a crockpot and heat through on low.
