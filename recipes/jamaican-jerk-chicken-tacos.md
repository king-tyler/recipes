# Jamaican Jerk Chicken Tacos

![](/recipes/images/jamaican-jerk-chicken-tacos.jpg)

## Ingredients

- **5 tsp** Jamaican jerk seasoning
- **3** chicken breasts, pounded to 1/2" thickness
- **2 cups** coleslaw
- **2 tbsp** cilantro
- **1/3 cup** mayo

## Directions

Season chicken breasts with 3 tsp. jerk seasoning, rubbing into both sides. Cook over medium heat
on the stove for 4-5 minutes on each side until cooked through. Slice into strips. Whisk together
mayo and 2 tsp. jerk seasoning.

Assemble tacos. Place coleslaw on each tortilla. Top with chicken, then drizzle with sauce. Top
with cilantro.
