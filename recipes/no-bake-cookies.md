# No-Bake Cookies

![](/recipes/images/no-bake-cookies.jpg)

## Ingredients

- **1/2 cup** butter, melted
- **2 cups** sugar
- **1/2 cups** milk
- **4 tbsp** cocoa powder
- **1/2 cup** creamy peanut butter
- **2 tsp** vanilla
- **5 cups** oats

## Directions

Add the butter, sugar, milk, and cocoa to a large, microwavable bowl. Microwave on high until
boiling and hold for 1 minute. 

Immediately, add peanut butter into the hot mixture and stir until melted. Add in vanilla. Mix in
the oats and drop by tablespoons onto wax paper. Let cool until set.
